RPG Maker project


I recently created my first RPG game using RPG Maker, diving into the world of game development as a beginner. The experience was both challenging and rewarding, as I learned the fundamentals of designing characters, crafting storylines, and building immersive environments. RPG Maker provided an intuitive platform that allowed me to bring my ideas to life without requiring advanced programming skills. From setting up battle mechanics to designing maps, each step taught me new creative and technical skills. This project not only sparked my passion for game development but also gave me a sense of accomplishment as I completed my very first game.
